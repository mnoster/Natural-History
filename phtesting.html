pH Testing

The pH scale is very useful in determining the quality of water. On the left side of the scale is ACIDIC and on the right is the BASIC. A pH level of 6.5-7.5 is going to be healthy for most organisms. A score of 7 is ideal for most of life. If the scale goes to 8+ or 6 and below, we can expect the water to be harmful to many living things. If the level changes even one or two points away from neutral (7), plants and fish will die.
