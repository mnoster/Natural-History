Turbidity and Dissolved Oxygen

Turbidity is the cloudiness of a liquid due to various types of particles such as dirt, pollutants, and organisms. Water with high turbidity usually indicates poor water quality. Similar to how smoke pollutes the air, water can be polluted by cars, boats, and trash. Humans are the number one polluters of water and air...but you can make difference!

Dissolved Oxygen

How does oxygen get into the water?

Dissolved Oxygen is the amount of oxygen present in the water. There are many ways for oxygen to get into the water such as algae releasing oxygen during photosynthesis, oxygen from the atmosphere, or rivers that trap air in the water when it flows.
